Description

The average busy time of Tsn(n=1~31) is calculated in the measurement period. When the Ts is released, the occupied time is calculated(time when release - time when occupied), and the number of samplings is increased by 1. At the end of the measurement period, the average busy time is calculated by dividing the sum of occupied time by the number of samplings.

Measurement point

The measurement is measured when the time-slot is released.
