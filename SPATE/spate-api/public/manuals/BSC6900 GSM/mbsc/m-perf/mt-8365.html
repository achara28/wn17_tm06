Description

The measurement of the preceding counters provides the number of times that the BTS reports the EMR or pre-processed EMR with the MEAN_BEP ranging from 0 to 31 over the TCHF.

Measurement point

After receiving an EMR or pre-processed EMR from the BTS over the TCHF, the BSC decodes the MEAN_BEP in the downlink MR, and the specific counter is incremented by one.
