Channel Assignment Failure Measurement per Cell(CHAN.AllocFail.CELL)

Overview

The measurement unit "Channel Assignment Failure Measurement per Cell" is used to measure the channel assignment failures due to busy or unconfigured channels when the BSC assigns the SDCCH, TCHF, or TCHH channel in the incoming internal inter-cell handover procedure.
