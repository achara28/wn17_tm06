RQI Measurement per TRX(MR.RQIDistrib.TRX)

Overview

This measurement unit provides the counters related to the RQI distribution on a TRX. Radio Quality Indication (RQI), reflects the voice quality in the radio network. The greater the RQI, the better the voice quality. The distribution of the voice quality on the TRX in the radio network can be learned through the counters in the measurement unit.
