Description

TA is short for Timing Advance, it indirectly reflects the distance between the MS and BTS. A larger value of TA indicates longer distance between the MS and BTS. RQI reflects the C/I ratio of a call. A measurement report contains RQI only when the call is on the AMR TRX.

During an AMR call, the MS periodically sends an MR with the RQI. After receiving the MR, the BSC decodes the TA in the RQI of the MR. A specified counter is measured based on the value of the TA. This measurement provides the distribution of the RQI based on the TA.

Measurement point

During a granularity period, the MR or pre-processed MR received by the BSC may contain RQI. In case of MR, the specified counter TRX.RQI.MR.NUM.BY.TA.N is incremented by one based on the value of the TA in the MR. In case of pre-processed MR, the specified counter TRX.RQI.MR.NUM.BY.TA.N is incremented by M based on the value of the TA in the pre-processed MR.

N indicates a value or a value range.If BSC received pre-processed MR,M = 1 maps the frequency of 2 per second; M = 2 maps the frequency of 1 per second; M = 4 maps the frequency of 0.5 per second; M = 8 maps the frequency of 0.25 per second
