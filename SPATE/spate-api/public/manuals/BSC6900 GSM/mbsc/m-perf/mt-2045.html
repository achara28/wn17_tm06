Channel Assignment Failures (All Channels Busy or Channels Unconfigured) in Assignment Procedure (TCH)

Measurement Counters

ID Counter Description
1278476420 CELL.ASS.CH.ALLOC.FAIL.TIMES.FR R3127B:Channel Assignment Failures (All Channels Busy or Channels Unconfigured) in Assignment Procedure (TCHF)
1278476421 CELL.ASS.CH.ALLOC.FAIL.TIMES.HR R3128B:Channel Assignment Failures (All Channels Busy or Channels Unconfigured) in Assignment Procedure (TCHH)

Description

The previous counters are used to measure the channel assignment failures due to busy or unconfigured channels when the BSC assigns the TCHFs or TCHHs in the assignment procedure.

Measurement point

This counter is measured in two cases. One is that the BSC fails to assign the TCHF, TCHH, TCHF or TCHH, TCHF priority, or TCHH priority channel as requested. The other is that the assignment is successful but the actual channel is not the requested channel.
