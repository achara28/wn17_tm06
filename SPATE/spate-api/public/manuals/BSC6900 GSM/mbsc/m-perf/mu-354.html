MEAN_BEP in EMR Measurement per TRX(MR.EmrMeanBep.TRX)

Overview

The measurement unit provides the MEAN_BEP contained in the EMR. Higher MEAN_BEP suggests lower BER.

MEAN_BEP refers to the mean bit error probability of the received signals. MEAN_BEP is obtained by the following way:The MS calculates the MEAN_BEP block of the correctly received and decoded radio block every four bursts.Obtain the MEAN_BEP by averaging the MEAN_BEP blocks in the whole granularity period.Then the logarithm of the MEAN_BEP to the base 10 is calculated and mapped onto a certain rank (0-31). The rank is reported.The EMR can be reported only when the EMR function is supported by all of the MS, BTS, and BSC.
