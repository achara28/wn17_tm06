Downlink VQI Measurement per TRX(MR.DownlinkVQI.TRX)

Overview

This measurement unit is used to measure the VQI provided by the BTS when the MS provides the EMR. It is used to measure the voice quality distribution. The VQI assesses the voice quality. The score ranges from 0 to 5, 5 indicates the best voice quality and 0 indicates the worst voice quality. The BSC measures the downlink VQI within 0.0-0.5, 0.5-1.0, 1.0-1.5, 1.5-2.0, 2.0-2.5, 2.5-3.0, 3.0-3.5, 3.5-4.0, 4.0-4.5, and 4.5-5.0 respectively.
