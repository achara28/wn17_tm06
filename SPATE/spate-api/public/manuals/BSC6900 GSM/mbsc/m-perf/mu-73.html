MR Measurement upon Handover per Cell(CALL.HoLevlQualTa.CELL)

Overview

The measurement unit "MR Measurement upon Handover per Cell" is used to measure the signal level, signal quality, and timing advance (TA) status in the MRs when a handover occurs.
