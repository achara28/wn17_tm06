Description

The BSC gets the uplink-and-downlink balance level from the valid MRs sent by the MS and the valid preprocessing MRs sent by the BTS. Then the BSC measures the number of MRs when the uplink-and-downlink balance level is from 1 to 11.

Measurement point

The Items are measured when:

The BSC receives valid MRs from the MS and gets the uplink-and-downlink level (1~11) from the MS .

The BSC receives valid preprocessing MRs from the BTS and gets the uplink-and-downlink level (1~11) from the MS .
