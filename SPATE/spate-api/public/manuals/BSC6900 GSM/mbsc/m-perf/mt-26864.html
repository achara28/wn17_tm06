Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests

Measurement Counters

ID Counter Description
1278079527 CELL.INTRABSC.OUTCELL.HO.REQ CH310:Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests

Description

The BSC uses the handover algorithm to decide whether a handover is required based on the measurement reports. If a handover is required, and if the originating cell (current cell) and the target cell are two different cells controlled by one BSC, the BSC initiates an internal inter-cell handover.

This counter provides the number of outgoing internal inter-cell handover requests initiated by the BSC. The measurement is performed based on the originating cell and involves handovers on the SDCCH, TCHF, and TCHH and handovers between frequency bands .

Measurement point

As shown in Figure 1, this counter is measured at A when the BSC decides that an outgoing internal inter-cell handover is required based on the measurement report.

Figure 1 Measurement of the counters related to outgoing internal inter-cell handover (excluding directed retry)

D Successful Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Excluding Directed Retry)

As shown in Figure 2, this counter is measured at A when the BSC decides the directed retry procedure is required during the assignment procedure and initiates an outgoing internal inter-cell handover based on the measurement report.

Formula

Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests = [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (SDCCH) (Excluding Directed Retry) (900/850/810-900/850/810)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (SDCCH) (Excluding Directed Retry) (1800/1900-1800/1900)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (SDCCH) (Excluding Directed Retry) (900/850/810-1800/1900)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (SDCCH) (Excluding Directed Retry) (1800/1900-900/850/810)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TCHF) (Excluding Directed Retry) (900/850/810-900/850/810)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TCHF) (Excluding Directed Retry) (1800/1900-1800/1900)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TCHF) (Excluding Directed Retry) (900/850/810-1800/1900)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TCHF) (Excluding Directed Retry) (1800/1900-900/850/810)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TCHH) (Excluding Directed Retry) (900/850/810-900/850/810)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TCHH) (Excluding Directed Retry) (1800/1900-1800/1900)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TCHH) (Excluding Directed Retry) (900/850/810-1800/1900)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TCHH) (Excluding Directed Retry) (1800/1900-900/850/810)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (Directed Retry) (900/850/810-900/850/810)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (Directed Retry) (1800/1900-1800/1900)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (Directed Retry) (900/850/810-1800/1900)] + [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (Directed Retry) (1800/1900-900/850/810)]
