Description

The measurements provide the number of successful intra-BSC and inter-BSC outgoing cell handovers initiated by the handover decision algorithm, based on different causes. For the outgoing cell handover caused by non-direct retry, the BSC measures the outgoing cell handover requests on receiving the handover request if handover retry is not allowed. If handover retry is allowed, the BSC measures the outgoing cell handover requests after sending the handover command. For the outgoing cell handover caused by direct retry, the BSC directly measures the outgoing cell handover requests on receiving the handover indication if the cause value is direct retry. The handover causes consist of the following:
  • uplink quality
  • downlink quality
  • uplink strength
  • downlink strength
  • timing advance
  • better cell
  • load
  • rapid level drop
  • MSC intervention
  • OM intervention
  • directed retry
  • other causes (including uplink intervention, downlink intervention, frequency offset handover, quick PBGT handover, fast movement handovers, and handover due to TFO speech version incompatibility).

Measurement point

For the measurement points, refer to different types of outgoing cell handover requests.
