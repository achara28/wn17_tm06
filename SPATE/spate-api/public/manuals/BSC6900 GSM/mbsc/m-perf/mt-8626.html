1282429662 TRX.EXT.WBAMR.SHORT.VQI.NUM S4504W:Number of the Measurements of the WBAMR UL VQI (Voice Quality Index) in the Customized MR

Description

This counter provides the number of times that the quality of WBAMR services carried on a TRX is reported in a measurement period. The VQI provides a method for direct measurement of the voice quality for radio network and establishes a corresponding relational model for the radio network performance and voice quality. Based on the radio quality parameters of the uplink and downlink voices, the VQI scores are calculated to determine and qualify the voice quality within the network, thus providing a basis for network optimization. The VQI employs the mean operating score (MOS) method of analysis to rate voice quality. MOS analysis is a subjective method for rating medium- and low-rate voice coding quality. MOS values ranges from 1 to 5.
  • 5: Excellent
  • 4: Good
  • 3: Fair
  • 2: Poor
  • 1: Unsatisfactory
VQI information is a Huawei's customized information element (IE) in the customized extended MR. BTS must support customized extended MRs for this to function. During each call, the BSC receives a customized extended MR or pre-processed MR report from the BTS every five measurement report periods (2.4 sec). These five measurement report periods (2.4 sec) are equal to one short measurement period. After receiving the customized extended MR, the BSC decodes and records the VQI IE. This counter is used to calculate the counter "Average Measurement Value of the WBAMR UL VQI (Voice Quality Index) in the Customized MR".

Measurement point

After receiving a customized extended MR or pre-processed customized extended MR of WBAM services, the BSC decodes the MR. This counter is incremented by one when the VQI contained in the MR is valid, that is, the VQI is not 255.
