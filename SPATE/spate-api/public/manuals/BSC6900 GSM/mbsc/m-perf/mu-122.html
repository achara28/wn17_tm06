Measurement of Maximum Busy Channels per Cell(CHAN.StateMaxNumOrig.CELL)

Overview

The Measurement of Maximum Busy Channels per Cell measurement unit provides the maximum number of busy SDCCHs and busy TCHs configured in a cell under the BSC in a measurement period. This number includes the number of channels that are dynamically converted. This measurement unit reflects the highest frequency of the MS random access in the cell in a measurement period in the form of the maximum number of busy channels.
