Failed Incoming External Inter-Cell Handovers (1800/1900-900/850/810)

Measurement Counters

ID Counter Description
1278078495 CELL.INTERBSC.IN.CELL.HO.FAIL.TIMES.1800.900 RH3034F:Failed Incoming External Inter-Cell Handovers (1800/1900-900/850/810)

Description

The incoming external inter-cell handover may fail due to various reasons. This counter provides the total number of failed incoming external inter-cell handovers initiated by the BSC. The measurement is performed in the target cell and covers failed handovers on the SDCCH and the TCH. In addition, the frequency band of the originating cell can be DCS1800 or PCS1900, and the frequency band of the target cell can be GSM900, GSM900&DCS1800, GSM850, GSM850&DCS1800, GSM850&PCS1900 or TGSM810.

Measurement point

See measurement points of the counters in the formula.

Formula

Failed Incoming External Inter-Cell Handovers (1800/1900-900/850/810) = [Failed Incoming External Inter-Cell Handovers (SDCCH) (1800/1900-900/850/810)] + [Failed Incoming External Inter-Cell Handovers (TCH) (1800/1900-900/850/810)]
