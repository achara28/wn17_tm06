Trx Power Adjust Off Duration

Measurement Counters

ID Counter Description
1276085417 TRX.PWR.ADJUST.OFF.DUR S4016A:Trx power adjust off duration

Description

This counter provides the duration of TRX turning off power amplifier in a measurement period. The BSC requires the BTS to turn off power amplifier by sending the corresponding command, and then the BTS measures the duration of TRX turning off power amplifier and periodically sends a corresponding test report to the BSC. The measurement is triggered when the BSC receives the test report from the BTS.

Measurement point

The BTS samples the shutdown duration of the power amplifier periodically and reports the sampled value to the BSC every 5 minutes in a measurement period. When a measurement period ends, the BSC accumulates the values reported by the BTS and obtains the value of this counter. The BTS sends the test report every 5 minutes, and therefore the accuracy of the interval depends on the preciseness of BTS time. However, the measurement period of the BSC is different from that of the BTS. Therefore, the reported value in a measurement period of the BSC may differ from that in actual conditions, and this is acceptable.
