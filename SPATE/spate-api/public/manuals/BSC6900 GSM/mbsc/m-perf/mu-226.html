Measurement related to FE or GE Ports on the BTS Transmission Board(BTSM.PTUFE)

Overview

This measurement unit allows the BTS to measures the packets transferred over the FE/GE port by category. Two FE ports are configured for an IP-based BTS. This helps the BTS measure the transferred packets and the customer get knowledge of the IP transmission quality. Only IP-based BTSs report the measurement information to the BSC for processing.
