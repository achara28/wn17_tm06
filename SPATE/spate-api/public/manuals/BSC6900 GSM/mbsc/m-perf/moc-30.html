Measurement of GSM Cell Performance(GCELL)

Overview

Measurement of GSM Cell Performance is used to measure the performance of the cell. Measurement results of handover- and assignment-related counters for this measurement object are recorded when a call is complete. The record time may be much later than the actual measurement time.
