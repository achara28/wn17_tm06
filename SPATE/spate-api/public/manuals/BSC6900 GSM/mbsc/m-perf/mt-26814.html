Number of Inter-RAT Outgoing Cell Handover Successes During BTS Local Switch

Measurement Counters

ID Counter Description
1278083484 CELL.INTERRAN.OUTCELL.HO.SUCC.BTS.LOOP CH353b:Number of Inter-RAT Outgoing Cell Handover Successes During BTS Local Switch

Description

This measurement provides the total number of inter-RAT outgoing cell handover successes during BTS local switching. It is calculated by subtracting the Number of Inter-System Outgoing Cell Handover Failures During BTS local switching from the Number of Inter-System Outgoing Cell Handover Requests During BTS local switching.

Measurement point

As shown in Figure 1 and Figure 2, the counter is measured at C when the BSC receives the CLEAR CMD message from the MSC during the outgoing inter-RAT inter-cell handover and the Cause is Handover successful (0x0B).

Figure 1 Measurement of the counters related to outgoing inter-RAT inter-cell handover (excluding directed retry)

Measurement points Description
A Measurement point of Outgoing Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Requests (Excluding Directed Retry)
B Measurement point of Outgoing Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Commands (Excluding Directed Retry)
C Measurement point of Successful Outgoing Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (Excluding Directed Retry)
Figure 2 Measurement of the counters related to outgoing inter-RAT inter-cell handover (directed retry)

Measurement points Description
A Measurement point of Outgoing Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Requests (Directed Retry)
B Measurement point of Outgoing Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Commands (Directed Retry)
C Measurement point of Successful Outgoing Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (Directed Retry)

Formula

Number of Inter-RAT Outgoing Cell Handover Successes During BTS Local Switch = [Number of Inter-RAT Outgoing Cell Handover Requests During BTS Local Switch] - [Number of Inter-RAT Outgoing Cell Handover Failures During BTS Local Switch]
