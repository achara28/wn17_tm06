Number of Handovers due to Power Deficit

1282411789 CELL.PWR.OVERLOAD.HO.FAIL.DROP.TIMERS R3197:Number of Call Drops in the Handover due to Power Deficit

Description

When the BSC detects that the power of a timeslot in a TRX group (the TRXs in the group share the power amplifiers in the same resource pool) is in deficit, the BSC hands over a call on the timeslot to a channel on another timeslot in the same cell, to a channel in another cell, to a channel in another BSC, or even to a channel in another system. These counters provide the number of handover requests due to power deficit, number of handover commands, number of successful handovers, and number of call drops due to handover failure.

Measurement point

When the BSC initiates a handover due to power deficit, "Number of Handover Requests due to Power Deficit" is measured, as shown at point A in Figure 1 and Figure 2. When a call drop occurs in handover due to power deficit, "Number of Call Drops in the Handover due to Power Deficit" is measured, as shown at points B and C in Figure 1 and point B in Figure 2. When the BSC sends a handover command due to power deficit to the MS, "Number of Handover Commands due to Power Deficit" is measured, as shown at point B in Figure 1 and Figure 2. When the handover due to power deficit is complete, "Number of Successful Handovers due to Power Deficit" is measured, as shown at point D in Figure 1 and point C in Figure 2.
