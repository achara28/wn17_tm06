Description

If the speech versions or rate sets used by the calling and called MSs are incompatible before the TFO function is enabled, an intra-BSC handover due to TFO speech version incompatibility is initiated to make them compatible and enable the TFO function, improving the speech quality. The following eight counters measure the number of internal intra-cell handover requests, handover commands, failed handovers and success rate of handovers due to TFO speech version incompatibility: H3006A: CELL.INTRACEL.HO.REQ.TIMES.WBAMR.WB.NWB H3006B: CELL.INTRACEL.HO.REQ.TIMES.WBAMR.NWB.WB H3016A: CELL.INTRACELL.HO.CMD.TIMES.WBAMR.WB.NWB H3016B: CELL.INTRACELL.HO.CMD.TIMES.WBAMR.NWB.WB H3026A: CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.TIMES.WBAMR.WB.NWB H3026B: CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.TIMES.WBAMR.NWB.WB TH3036A: CELL.INTRACELL.HO.SUCC.RATE.WBAMR.WB.TO.NWB TH3036B: CELL.INTRACELL.HO.SUCC.RATE.WBAMR.NWB.TO.WB However, with the gradual improvement of the TFO function, the current handovers include both internal intra-cell handovers and internal inter-cell handovers, both WBAMR-NBAMR handovers and those between other speech versions. The eight counters cannot provide reliable and complete statistics for handovers due to TFO speech version incompatibility. Therefore, these counters are no longer useful. The following counters are added to measure the number of internal intra-cell or inter-cell handover requests, handover commands, failed handovers and success rate of handovers due to TFO speech version incompatibility: A3408:CELL.TRAU.TFO.HO.REQ.TIMES.INCOMP A3409:CELL.TRAU.TFO.HO.CMD.TIMES.INCOMP A3410:CELL.TRAU.TFO.HO.FAIL.TIMES.INCOMP RA3408:CELL.TRAU.TFO.HO.SUCC.RATE.INCOMP.
