Description

The counters provide the numbers of failed internal intra-cell handovers based on the type of the channel the MS seizes, the handover failure cause, and the handover reason. They can be combined with the Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests and the Internal Intra-Cell Handover Commands to reflect the performance of internal intra-cell handovers.

The counter H3020 indicates the number of failed internal intra-cell handovers when the target channel is an SDCCH. The counter H3029 indicates the number of failed internal intra-cell handovers when the target channel is a TCH. They cover the number of failed internal intra-cell handovers due to various reasons.
  • H3020:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.SD
  • H3029:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.TCH
The following counters indicate the numbers of failed internal intra-cell handovers because of no proper speech version is available
  • H302H:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.NO.SPEECH.VER
The following counters indicate the numbers of failed internal intra-cell handovers based on various handover failure causes.
1) The following counters indicate the numbers of failed internal intra-cell handovers due to the allocation failure of resources.
  • H3020A:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.CONG.SD
  • H3029A:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.CONG.TCH
The following counter provides the number of failed internal intra-cell handovers when the BSC enables the dynamic allocation of Abis resources but fails to allocate an Abis circuit in the target cell (current cell).
  • H302E: CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.NO.IDLE.ABIS
The following counter provides the number of failed internal intra-cell handovers when the BSC fails to obtain the requested circuit during the circuit connection process.
  • H302B:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.APPLY.TRSL.FAIL
2) The following counters indicate the numbers of failed internal intra-cell handovers when the MS fails to access the new channel on the air interface.
In the internal intra-cell handover procedure, the BSC sends an HO CMD message to the MS after assigning a channel in the target cell (current cell) and successfully activating the channel and then starts a timer for waiting for an HO CMP message. The following counters provide the numbers of failed internal intra-cell handovers due to the expiry of the timer for waiting for an HO CMP message based on the target channel type and the service type (SDCCH, TCHF signaling channel, TCHH signaling channel, TCHF traffic channel, or TCHH traffic channel).
  • H3020C:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.EXP.SD
  • H3027Cb:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.EXP.TCHF.SIG
  • H3028Cb:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.EXP.TCHH.SIG
  • H3027Ca:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.EXP.TCHF.TRAF
  • H3028Ca:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.EXP.TCHH.TRAF
In the internal intra-cell handover procedure, the BSC sends an HO CMD message to the MS after assigning a channel in the target cell (current cell) and successfully activating the channel. The above counters provide the numbers of failed internal intra-cell handovers based on the handover failure reason reported by the MS when the MS fails to access the new channel and then reconnects to the old channel. The cause values for the MS to reconnect to the old channel after failing to access the new channel are as follows:
  • Abnormal release, unspecified
  • Abnormal release, channel unacceptable
  • Abnormal release, timer expired
  • Abnormal release, no activity on the radio path
  • Preemptive release
  • Handover impossible, timing advance out of range
  • Channel mode unavailable
  • Frequency not implemented
  • Call already cleared
  • Semantically incorrect message
  • Invalid mandatory information
  • Message type non-existent or not implemented
  • Message type not compatible with protocol state
  • Conditional IE error
  • Cell allocation available
  • Protocol error unspecified
  • Other
The following counters indicate the above reason of failed internal intra-cell handovers.

3) The following counters indicate the numbers of failed internal intra-cell handovers due to the abnormality of devices. The following counter provides the number of failed internal intra-cell handovers when the BSC locally releases the call after receiving an SS7 link abnormality indication in the internal intra-cell handover procedure.
  • H302G:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.A.INTERF.FAIL
The following counters indicate the numbers of failed internal intra-cell handovers based on various handover reasons.
a) The following counters indicate the numbers of failed internal intra-cell handovers when the handover reason is a handover from the overlaid of a concentric cell to the underlaid of the concentric cell or from the underlaid of a concentric cell to the overlaid of the concentric cell.
  • H3022A:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.CONG.ULCELL.OLCELL
  • H3021A:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.CONG.OLCELL.ULCELL
The counter H3022M provides the number of failed internal intra-cell handovers when a handover from the underlaid of a concentric cell to the overlaid of the concentric cell fails due to other reasons than no available channel. The counter H3021M provides the number of failed internal intra-cell handovers when a handover from the overlaid of a concentric cell to the underlaid of the concentric cell fails due to other reasons than no available channel.
  • H3022M:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.OTHERS.ULCELL.OLCELL
  • H3021M:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.OTHERS.OLCELL.ULCELL
b) The counter H302a indicates the number of failed internal intra-cell handovers when the handover reason is the local switching of the BSC. The counter H302 indicates the number of failed internal intra-cell handovers when the handover reason is the local switching of the BTS.
  • H302a:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.BSC.LOOP
  • H302: CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.BTS.LOOP
c) The counter H3025A indicates the number of failed internal intra-cell handovers when the handover reason is a TCHF-TCHH conversion for an AMR call. The counter H3025B indicates the number of failed internal intra-cell handovers when the handover reason is a TCHH-TCHF conversion for an AMR call.
  • H3025A:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.AMR.TCHF.TCHH
  • H3025B:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.AMR.TCHH.TCHF

As shown in Figure 1, each time the internal intra-cell handover fails when no circuit is available, the circuit connection fails, or the assigned channel fails to be activated (see measurement point B), one of the following counters is incremented by one.
  • H302B:CELL.INTRACELL.HO.FAIL.APPLY.TRSL.FAIL
As shown in Figure 1, the BSC starts a timer for waiting for an HO CMP message after sending an HO CMD message in the internal intra-cell handover procedure. Each time the timer expires (see measurement point C), one of the following counters is incremented by one.
