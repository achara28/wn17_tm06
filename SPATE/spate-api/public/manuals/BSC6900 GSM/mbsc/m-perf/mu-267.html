BTS Energy Measurement(BTSM.EnergySrc.Site)

Overview

This measurement unit provides the measurement of energy counters of a BTS, including the voltage, current, and power generation capacity of the solar panel, blower, diesel engine, and storage battery. This measurement unit provides specific information about the energy at a site in real time. This measurement unit can be measured only for the site that is configured with the solar energy controller.
