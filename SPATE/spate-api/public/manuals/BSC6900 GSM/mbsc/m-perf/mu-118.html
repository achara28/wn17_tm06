Channel Configuration Measurement per Cell(CHAN.CfgNum.CELL)

Overview

During operation, the BSC dynamically adjusts the channel resources, such as TCHs, SDCCHs, and PDCHs, for optimal use of channels. The BSC also provides the statistics related to the status of channels on a regular basis. The status is busy, idle, or blocked. These data helps to provide the information on the system operation and on the service distribution. The BSC periodically samples the channel allocation data, such as the number of configured channels and the number of available channels. The sampling timer is set to five seconds.
