Description

The measurement of the preceding counters provides the number of times that the BTS reports the EMR or pre-processed EMR with the CV_BEP ranging from 0 to 7 over the TCHH.

Measurement point

After receiving an EMR or pre-processed EMR from the BTS over the TCHH, the BSC decodes the CV_BEP in the downlink MR, and the specific counter is incremented by one.
