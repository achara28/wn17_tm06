Description

CR3557:CELL.TRAF.CH.TRAF.ULCELL refers to Traffic Volume of TCHs (Underlaid Subcell), that is, the average number of busy TCHs of the underlaid subcell.

CR3558:CELL.TRAF.CH.TRAF.OLCELL refers to Traffic Volume of TCHs (Overlaid Subcell), that is, the average number of busy TCHs of the overlaid subcell.

CR355A:CELL.TRAF.CH.TRAF refers to Traffic Volume of TCHs, that is, the average number of busy TCHs.

Measurement point

See measurement points of the counters in the formula.

Formula

Traffic Volume on TCHs (Underlaid Subcell) = Mean Number of Busy TCHs (Underlaid Subcell)

Traffic Volume of TCHs (Overlaid Subcell) = [Mean Number of Busy TCHs (Overlaid Subcell)]

Traffic Volume of TCHs = [Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHF) (900/850/810 band)] + [Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHF) (1800/1900 Band)] + [Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHH) (900/850/810 band)] + [Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHH) (1800/1900 Band)]
