Logical Port Measurement(LOGIC.PORT)

Overview

The logic ports measurement helps monitor the transmission quality of the running system and analyze system transmission quality to maintain system stability and reliability. A logical port performs shaping and reverse pressure on the frames at the data link layer, including MAC frames, PPP frames, and MP frames. Thus, the logical port belongs to the data link layer.
