Customized MRs per TRX(MR.ExtMrOrig.TRX)

Overview

This measurement unit provides the counters related to the customized extended measurement reports in a cell.

To provide more information about the BTS Um interface, the following information is added in the customized extended measurement report: main and diversity level, VQI, and number of frames with various rates. If the BTS supports the customized extended measurement report function, the measurement reports or pre-processed measurement reports sent by the BTS contain the customized extended cells. There is no switch on the BTS and BSC to determine whether the cells are contained in the measurement reports. These cells in the customized extended measurement reports are defined by Huawei, they are not defined in standard protocols.

If the BTS supports the customized extended measurement report function, then in each call, the BSC receives the measurement reports or pre-processed measurement reports containing the customized extended cells sent by the BTS every five measurement report periods (2.4s). These measurement reports or pre-processed measurement reports may contain the main and diversity level, VQI, and number of frames with various rates. To enable the BTS to report the voice quality index (VQI), set the Report Speech Quality parameter to YES(Report) on the LMT. After the BTS reports the VQI, the BSC collects the traffic statistics on a per VQI basis. There are 11 levels of speech quality. If the level is higher, the speech quality is better. The traffic related to AMR and non-AMR is measured separately, and thus the speech quality is monitored.
