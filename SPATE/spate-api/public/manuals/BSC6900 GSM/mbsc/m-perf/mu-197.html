LAPD Link Measurement at the BTS(BTSM.Lapd)

Overview

This measurement unit is used to measure the number of various frames related to LAPD links on the BTS side. The start time and measurement period for LAPD links on the BSC side are different from those on the BTS side. As a result, the measurement results of the LAPD links on the BSC side are slightly different from those on the BTS side. The OML_LAPD, ESL_LAPD, and EML_LAPD links on the BSC side are measured after the command for activating the BTS is delivered successfully, and the RSL_LAPD link is measured after TRXs are activated successfully. The measurement period is 5 minutes. The OML_LAPD, ESL_LAPD, and EML_LAPD links on the BTS side are measured after the BTS is started, and the RSL_LAPD link is measured after TRXs receive configuration data. The measurement period is 4 minutes.
