FEGE Ethernet Port Measurement(FEGE)

Overview

FEGE Ethernet ports are important resources of the Base Station Controller. Therefore, the FEGE Ethernet ports can be measured for the analysis of the transmission quality of the running Base Station Controller so that the stable and reliable operation of the Base Station Controller can be maintained.

Figure 1 The four layers of the TCP/IP protocol suite

FEGE Ethernet port belongs to the Link layer of the TCP/IP protocol suite, as figure 1 shows.

Figure 2 Physical bearer of the FEGE Ethernet Port

In the configuration structure, FE/GE ports are physical ports, as shown in the figure 2.
