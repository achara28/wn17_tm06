Radio Link Failure Measurement per TRX(MR.DropAccu.TRX)

Overview

The measurement unit provides the level, quality, and TA contained in the measurement report during radio link failure (when the BSC receives the ERR IND, CONN FAIL IND, and REL IND messages).
