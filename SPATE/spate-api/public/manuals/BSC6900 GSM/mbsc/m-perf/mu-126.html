Speech Frame in Customized MR per Cell(MR.ExtMrFrameNum.CELL)

Overview

This measurement unit (Speech Frame in Customized MR per Cell) provides the counters related to the user-defined extended measurement reports in a cell. In each call, the TRX receives user-defined extended measurement reports reported by the DSP every five reporting periods (2.4s). The user-defined extended measurement reports may contain the main/diversity level, VQI, and frames of various rates. When the BTS reports the measurement results or pre-processing measurement results, the uplink measurement report contains these values. To enable the BTS to report the voice quality index (VQI), set the Report Speech Quality parameter to YES(Report) on the LMT. After the BTS reports the VQI, the BSC collects the traffic statistics on a per VQI basis. There are 11 levels of speech quality. If the level is higher, the speech quality is better. The traffic related to AMR and non-AMR is measured separately, and thus the speech quality is monitored.
