Description

After the originating BSC makes the handover decision, the originating BSC analyzes the reason for the handover. This measurement provides the number of handovers for frequency shift. Along with Successful VGCS TCH Handovers for Frequency Shift, this measurement reflects the performance of the handovers for frequency shift.

Measurement point

The measurement point is specified in the following three situations:

(1) In the case of the intra-cell handover, this counter is incremented by one when the originating BSC sends a HANDOVER REQUIRED message to the MSC for frequency shift. The measurement point is A, as shown in Figure 1.

(2) In the case of the inter-cell handover, this counter is incremented by one when the BSC determines to initiate the internal inter-cell handover based on the measurement report for frequency shift.The measurement point is A, as shown in Figure 2.

(3) In the case of the inter-BSC handover, this counter is incremented by one when the originating BSC sends a HANDOVER_REQUIRED message to the MSC for frequency shift. The measurement point is A, as shown in Figure 3.
