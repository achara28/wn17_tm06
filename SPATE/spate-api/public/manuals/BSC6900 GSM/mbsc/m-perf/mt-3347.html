Strength of Signals in Neighbor Cells

Measurement Counters

ID Counter Description
1278272417 CELL.NCELL.LEV S360:Strength of Signals in Neighbor Cells

Description

After receiving a system message with the frequency configured in the BA table of the BSC as a neighbor cell of the present cell, the MS measures the information of this neighbor cell (including the BSIC, BCCH, and receive level, where the BSIC and BCCH uniquely identify a neighbor cell), and sends the information to the BTS in a measurement report. The BTS then sends the information to the BSC in a measurement report or a pre-processed measurement report.

After receiving the valid measurement report from the BTS, the BSC decodes the BSIC, BCCH, and receive level, and then measures the receive level of that neighbor cell.

Measurement point

After receiving the valid MR from the BTS in the serving cell, the BSC checks whether the defined neighbor cell in the MR is recorded based on the BSIC and BCCH frequency. If the cell is recorded, the downlink receive level of this cell is accumulated and the number of MRs is incremented by one. Otherwise, the BSC records the cell, and measures the BSIC, BCCH, receive level, and number of MRs of this cell.

The measurement object of this counter is configurable. If the measurement object is not configured, this counter is not measured.

Formula

None

Unit

dB
