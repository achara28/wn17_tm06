Measurement of Transmission Resource Pool IP Address RTP Performance(IPPOOL.RTP)

Overview

The support of IP-based IuCS/A interface is an important feature. In order to control the transmission quality on the Iu/A interface, the performance of IP paths is measured on the basis of Real-time Transport Protocol (RTP) and Real-time Transport Control Protocol (RTCP) packets. The transmission quality of the Source and Destination IP Addresses(IP Address Pair) is related to the measurement items such as Round Trip Time (RTT) of packets, delay jitter of packets, and packet loss rate, In order to increase the ability of surveillance.
