Description

When the BSC receives a valid measurement report or a pre-processed measurement report, and the parameter "FER Report Enable" is set to YES, the corresponding counter is measured on the basis of the FER and the receive quality indicated in the measurement report. In the measurement report, if the downlink FER is smaller than "FER threshold 1" and the downlink quality level is n, then the counter, Number of MRs on Downlink FER (Rank 0 and Receive Quality Rank n), is measured. If the downlink FER is greater than or equal to "FER threshold i" and smaller than "FER threshold i+1", and the downlink quality level is n, then the counter, Number of MRs on Downlink FER (Rank i and Receive Quality Rank n) is measured. If the downlink FER is greater than or equal to "FER threshold 7" and the downlink quality level is n, then the counter, Number of MRs on Downlink FER (Rank 7 and Receive Quality Rank n), is measured.

Measurement point

When the BSC receives the valid measurement report or pre-processed measurement report from the BTS in the serving cell and the parameter "FER Report Enable" is set to YES, the corresponding counter is measured on the basis of the FER and the receive quality indicated in the measurement report.
