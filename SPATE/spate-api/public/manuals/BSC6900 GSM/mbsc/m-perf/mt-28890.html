Description

This measurement provides the number of the measurements of the average downlink VQI in the MR or pre-processed MR based on various speech versions (full-rate speech version, half-rate speech version, enhanced full-rate speech version, AMR full-rate speech version, AMR half-rate speech version, and Wide Band AMR full-rate speech version).

Measurement point

The specific counter is incremented by one according to current speech version (such as, full-rate speech version, half-rate speech version, enhanced full-rate speech version, AMR full-rate speech version, AMR half-rate speech version, and Wide Band AMR full-rate speech version) when the BSC receives the pre-processed MR or original MR, and the downlink VQI is contained in the extended information.
