Description

This counter provides the duration of no SDCCH, TCH, TCHF, and TCHH for assignment in a cell controlled by the BSC in the granularity period. At the sampling points of the channel assignment measurement, if all the SDCCH, TCH, TCHF, or TCHH channel resources in a cell cannot be assigned because of busy or unavailable, the duration of the previous counters are accumulated based on the channel type.

Measurement point

At the sampling points of the channel assignment measurement, if the SDCCH, TCH, TCHF, or TCHH of all the cells are scanned but no channel is available for assignment, the corresponding counter is measured.
