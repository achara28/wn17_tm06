LAPD Link Measurement(LAPD.LINK)

Overview

LAPD link measurement provides the counters related to number of various Q921 frames transferred through the LAPD link at the BSC side. The LAPD links are the signaling channel over the Abis interface between the BSC and the BTS, the signaling channel over the Pb interface between the BSC and the PCU, and the signal channel over the Ater interface between the BM subrack and TC subrack of the BSC.
