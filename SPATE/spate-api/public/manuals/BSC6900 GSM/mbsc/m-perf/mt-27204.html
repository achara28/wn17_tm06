Description

This measurement provides the average throughput of downlink GPRS interactive service THPx LLC PDUs in a granularity period.

Measurement point

See measurement points of the counters in the formula.

Formula

Average Throughput of Downlink GPRS Interactive Service THP1 LLC Layer = [Throughput of Downlink GPRS Interactive Service THP1 LLC PDU]/([Total Bytes of Downlink GPRS Interactive Service THP1 LLC PDUs Sent] x 8/1024)

The formula of "Average Throughput of Downlink GPRS Interactive Service THPx LLC Layer" is similar as "[Average Throughput of Downlink GPRS Interactive Service THP1 LLC Layer]".

Note: THPx indicates THP1-THP3.

Unit

kbit/s
