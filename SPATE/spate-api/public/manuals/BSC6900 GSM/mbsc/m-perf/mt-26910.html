Handover Drop Rate of TCH

Measurement Counters

ID Counter Description
1278077525 CELL.TCH.HO.DROP RH333:Handover Drop Rate of TCH

Description

This counter provides the rate of TCH releases due to call drop on the Um interface during the handover to the TCH seizures in a cell. It is the rate of the sum of the failed internal intra-cell handovers (timer expired), the failed outgoing internal inter-cell handovers (timer expired), and the failed outgoing external inter-cell handovers to the sum of the successful TCH seizures in the internal intra-cell handover procedure (immediate assignment), the successful TCH seizures in the internal intra-cell handover procedure (assignment), the successful TCH seizures in the incoming internal inter-cell handover procedure, and the successful TCH seizures in the incoming external inter-cell handover procedure.

Measurement point

See measurement points of the counters in the formula.

Formula

Handover Drop Rate of TCH = ([Call Drops on Signal Channel (TCH)]+ [Call Drops in TCH Handovers (Traffic Channel)])x{100} / ([Successful TCH Seizures (Signaling Channel)]+ [Successful TCH Seizures (Traffic Channel)]+ [Successful TCH Seizures in TCH handovers (Traffic Channel)])
