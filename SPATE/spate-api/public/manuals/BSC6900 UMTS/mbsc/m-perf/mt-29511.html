Description

These counters provide the average uplink rates of different PS R99 background services in the cells in the active set.

Measurement point

The UL traffic (not including the RLC header and the retransmitted data, unit: bit) and the actual duration (unit: ms) with data transmission of each kind of PS R99 background services are accumulated in the measurement period for the cells that are under the SRNC and are in the active set. At the end of the measurement period, the value of this counter is obtained.

Formula

None

Unit

kbit/s
