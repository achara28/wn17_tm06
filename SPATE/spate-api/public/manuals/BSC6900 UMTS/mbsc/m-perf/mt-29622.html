Mean Throughput of a Single HSDPA MAC-d Flow in the Case of Multi-RAB Services for Cell

Measurement Counters

ID Counter Description
73439942 VS.MultiRAB.MeanThroughput.HSDPA Mean Throughput of a Single HSDPA MAC-d Flow in the Case of Multi-RAB Services for Cell

Description

This counter provides the mean throughput of a single HSDPA MAC-d flow in the case of Multi-RAB services in the HSDPA cell. For DC-HSDPA users, this counter is measured only in the primary cell based on the total throughput on the primary and secondary carriers.

Measurement point

When users initiate Multi-RAB services in an HSDPA cell and there is data transmission in the HSDPA cell, the total period of time used for transmitting data and the total number of bits transmitted in the HSDPA cell are calculated. At the end of the measurement period, the mean throughput of a single MAC-d flow at the RLC layer in a cell is calculated by dividing the total number of bits by the total period of time. Note that the total number of measured bits excludes RLC headers and retransmitted data.

Formula

None

Unit

kbit/s
