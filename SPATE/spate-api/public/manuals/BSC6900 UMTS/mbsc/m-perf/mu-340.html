Measurement of Trunk Group Performance(Trunk)

Overview

Trunk groups are important resources of the Base Station Controller. Therefore, the Trunk groups can be measured for the analysis of the transmission quality of the running Base Station Controller so that the stable and reliable operation of the Base Station Controller can be maintained.

Figure 1 The four layers of the TCP/IP protocol suite

Trunk group belongs to the Link layer of the TCP/IP protocol suite, as figure 1 shows.

Figure 2 Physical bearer of the TRUNK

In the configuration structure, trunks are between physical ports and logical applications, as shown in the figure 2.
