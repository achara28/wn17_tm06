Fractional IMA Link Measurement(FRACIMALNK)

Overview

The quality of transmission links directly affects the system performance. Fractional IMA Link measurement helps analyze system transmission quality so as to maintain system stability and reliability.

Figure 1 Physical bearer of the Fractional IMA

In the configuration structure, Fractional IMA links are between physical ports and logical applications, as shown in the figure 1.
