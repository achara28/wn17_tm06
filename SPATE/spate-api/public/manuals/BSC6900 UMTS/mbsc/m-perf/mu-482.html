Cell Measurement on BOOST(BOOST.CELL)

Overview

The BOOST.CELL measurement unit includes the numbers of UU BOOST service establishments and releases for UMTS-UMTS dual terminal in the downlink, and indicators related to UU BOOST traffic. This measurement unit is effective only when the UMTS-UMTS Dual Terminal Downlink BOOST feature is enabled.
