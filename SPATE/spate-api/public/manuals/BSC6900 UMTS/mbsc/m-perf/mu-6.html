QAAL2 Measurement(QAAL2)

Overview

The QAAL2 measurement helps monitor the ATM Adaptation Layer 2 (AAL2) connection and release to QAAL2 adjacent nodes. QAAL2 is the control plane protocol of AAL2 to control the setup and release of AAL2 connections. QAAL2 adjacent nodes of an RNC include NodeB, MSC and neighboring RNC (NRNC).
