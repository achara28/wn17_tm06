Description

These measurement items take statistics of the number of PS RABs requested to establish at different maximum downlink bit rates in the RNC according to different types of services.

Measurement point

The measurement is triggered at point A as shown in the figure. When the RNC receives from the SGSN a RAB ASSIGNMENT REQUEST message about the PS domain, the RNC measures these items according to different types of services.
