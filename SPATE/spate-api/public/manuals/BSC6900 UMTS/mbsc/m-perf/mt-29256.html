67204243 VS.MBMS.PTM.Channel4.Dur.Cell Duration of MBMS Channel 4 in PTM Mode for Cell

Description

The previous measurement counter provides the duration in which MBMS channel is in PTM mode.

Measurement point

When the MBMS channel is initially decided to be in PTM mode or switches from PTP to PTM, the system records the time as T1. When the MBMS channel stops or switches to PTP, the system records the time as T2. The difference between T1 and T2 indicates a duration in which the MBMS channel is in PTM mode. Assume that T is the difference between T1 and T2. The sum of all the Ts in the measurement period equals the total duration in which the MBMS channel is in PTM mode in the measurement period.
