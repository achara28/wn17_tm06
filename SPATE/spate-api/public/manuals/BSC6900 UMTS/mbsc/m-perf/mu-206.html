MP Group Measurement(MLPPP)

Overview

Transmission links are important resources of the system. The transmission quality of the transmission links directly affects the operation of any function of the system. Therefore, the traffic on MP groups can be measured for the analysis of the transmission quality of the running system so that the stable and reliable operation of the system can be maintained.

Figure 1 The four layers of the TCP/IP protocol suite

MP group belongs to the Link layer of the TCP/IP protocol suite, as figure 1 shows.
