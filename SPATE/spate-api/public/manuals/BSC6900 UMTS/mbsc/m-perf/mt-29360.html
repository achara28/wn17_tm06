Description

These counters provide the average number of RBs for PS R99 Background services with different UL and DL rates in the cells in the active set.

Measurement point

The RNC takes a sample every 5 seconds from the number of PS background service RBs at different bit rates. At the end of the measurement period, the RNC divides the accumulated number of background service RBs at each bit rate by the number of samples to obtain the mean number of PS background service RBs. In this case, the RNC measures the item in each cell that the UE camps on.
