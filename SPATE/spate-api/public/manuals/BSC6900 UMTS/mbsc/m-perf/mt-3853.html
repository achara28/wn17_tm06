Description

The measurement counters provide the number of Redirect in the scenario of the MOCN for different causes.

Measurement point

The measurement is triggered at point B shown in Figure 1. When the RNC receives from the CN a DIRECT TRANSFER message with the Redirection Indication IE, the corresponding counter is measured on the basis of the cause value carried in the Redirection Indication IE.
