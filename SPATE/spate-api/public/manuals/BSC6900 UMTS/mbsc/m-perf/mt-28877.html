Description

These counters provide the number of frames that are transmitted, received, or discarded by the trunk group in the measurement period. They indicate the traffic volume on the trunk group. The frames measured here are MAC frames

Measurement point

Samples are taken every five seconds in the specified measurement period. The system obtains the number of frames that are received, transmitted, and discarded on the port every five seconds, calculates the total number of frames that are received, transmitted, or discarded in one measurement period.
