IMA Group Measurement(IMAGRP)

Overview

The quality of transmission links directly affects the system performance. IMA group measurement helps analyze system transmission quality so as to maintain system stability and reliability.

Figure 1 ATM Reference Model

IMA group belongs to the physical layer of the ATM reference model, as figure 1 shows.

Figure 2 Physical bearer of the IMA Group

In the configuration structure, IMA groups are between physical ports and logical applications, as shown in the figure 2.
