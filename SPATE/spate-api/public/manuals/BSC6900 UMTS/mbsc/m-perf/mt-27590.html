Measurement of the Forward Bandwidth or Backward Bandwidth Assigned to an MLPPP Group

Measurement Counters

ID Counter Description

67204782 VS.MLPPP.Alloced.Ave.Bwd T7323:Mean backward bandwidth assigned to an MLPPP group
67195948 VS.MLPPP.Alloced.Max.Fwd T7326:Maximum Forward bandwidth assigned to an MLPPP group
67195949 VS.MLPPP.Alloced.Max.Bwd T7327:Maximum Backward bandwidth assigned to an MLPPP group

Description

These counters provide the forward bandwidth or backward bandwidth assigned to an MLPPP group from different perspectives.

Measurement point

In the measurement period, the system automatically takes samples on the forward bandwidth and backward bandwidth assigned to an MLPPP group every 15 seconds. At the end of the measurement period, the maximum forward bandwidth and the maximum backward bandwidth assigned to an MLPPP group in the measurement period are obtained. The mean forward bandwidth assigned to an MLPPP group equals to the accumulated value of the forward bandwidth at the sampling points divided by the number of sampling times. The mean backward bandwidth assigned to an MLPPP group equals to the accumulated value of the backward bandwidth at the sampling points divided by the number of sampling times.
