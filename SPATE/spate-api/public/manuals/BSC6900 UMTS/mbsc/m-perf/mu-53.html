Traffic Measurement of CN Operator per RNC(TRAFF.LOAD.PLMN.RNC)

Overview

Traffic Measurement for each Operator is used to measure the traffic of CS domain and PS domain of RNC for each Operator, and it contains the case of SHO. Where, the traffic of CS domain is denoted by equivalent Erlang; the traffic of PS domain is expressed in kbit/s.
