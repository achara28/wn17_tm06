Cell TX and RX Power Measurement per cell(RTWPTCP.Cell)

Overview

If a NodeB is configured to measure the transmit (TX) and receive (RX) power after cell setup, it shall periodically send the RNC a COMMON MEASUREMENT REPORT about the Received Total Wideband Power (RTWP) and another report about the total Transmitted Carrier Power (TCP). The cell RX/TX measurement unit calculates the RX and TX power of a cell.
