Measurement related to SIRTARGET per cell(SIR.Cell)

Overview

The Signal-to-Interference Ratio (SIR) target value used in the uplink out loop power control on the DCH is adjusted dynamically based on the uplink Block Error Rate (BLER) or Bit Error Rate (BER). The uplink outer loop power control adjusts the transmit power of the UE to lower the interference on other UEs while ensuring the service quality of the UE.

Based on different service classes and traffic rates, this measurement indicates the ratio of the duration when the target SIR reaches the maximum value to the total period when the uplink outer loop power control is performed.
