Measurement related to U2L Handover per cell(U2LTE.HO.Cell)

Overview

According to handover methods, outgoing UMTS-to-LTE PS handovers can be classified into PS inter-RAT handovers and redirection triggered by RRC connection release. Counters related to the number of failed handovers during different phases of outgoing handovers are measured based on different handover causes.
