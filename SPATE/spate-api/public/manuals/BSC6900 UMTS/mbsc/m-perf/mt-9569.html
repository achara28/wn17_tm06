Signaling Delay of RRC Established on EFACH in a Cell

Measurement Counters

ID Counter Description
73421757 VS.RRC.ConnEstabTimeMax.CellFACH.EFACH Maximum Duration of RRC Establishment Process on EFACH for Cell
73439900 VS.RRC.ConnEstabTimeMean.CellFACH.EFACH Mean Duration of RRC Establishment Process on EFACH for Cell

Description

The measurement items provide the delay of RRC signaling on the EFACH in a cell under the RNC.

Measurement point

The measurement is triggered at points A and C, as shown in the figure: At point A, the RNC records the time (T1) when it receives a RRC CONNECTION REQUEST message. At point C, the RNC records the time (T2) when it receives a RRC CONNECTION SETUP COMPLETE message. Suppose delta T represents the signaling delay of the RRC signaling connection setup on the EFACH in a cell. Then, delta T = T2 - T1.

Figure 1 RRC connection setup success flow
