Measurement related to NAS signaling(NAS.IU)

Overview

The measurement unit Non-Access Stratum (NAS) Signaling Measurement on the Iu Interface measures the number of NAS interactions on the Iu interface. The method of measuring NAS-related counters on the RNC are different from those on the CN,and the later prevails.
