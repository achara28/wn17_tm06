IPoA Link Measurement(IPOALNK)

Overview

Transmission links are important resources of the Base Station Controller system. The quality of the transmission links directly affects the system performance. Therefore, you can take statistics of the traffic in the ATM layer on the transmission links based on the IPoA link measurement. This is to analyze the transmission quality of and maintain the stable and secure operation of the system.
