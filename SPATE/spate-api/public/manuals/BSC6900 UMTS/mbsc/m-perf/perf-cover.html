Table 1 Terms
Term Explanation

Classification of measurement object

On the top level, measurement objects are classified into three categories: Radio Network Related Measurement, Transport Network Related Measurement, Hardware Platform Related Measurement.

Measurement object

Measurement objects are located at the second level of the document structure.

One measurement object contains one or more measurement units.

Measurement unit

Measurement units are located at the third level of the document structure.

One measurement unit contains one or more counters but belongs to only one measurement object.

Counter

Counters are located at the fourth level of the document structure.

Counters are the basic elements of performance measurement. One counter can belong to only one measurement unit.

Counter Relationship

A table that lists parent counters and their child counters in a table. Note that the child counters listed in the table cover only typical scenarios. That is, some scenarios are not covered by any counter. Therefore, the value of a parent counter is larger than or equal to the sum of the values of its child counters. Some counters may not be involved in any counter relationship.
