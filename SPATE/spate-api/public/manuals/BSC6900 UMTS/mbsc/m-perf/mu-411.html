Traffic Measurement per cell(TRFFIC.Cell)

Overview

This measurement unit involves the items related to the traffic on the common transport channels (PCH, RACH, and FACH) and on the dedicated transport channels on the Iub Interface. The measurement unit also involves the items about soft handover. The traffic on the dedicated transport channels are measured on the basis of the service type.
