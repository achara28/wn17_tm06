Measurement related to RTP flux in IPPATH(IPPATH.RTP)

Overview

The support of IP-based Iu/A interface is an important feature. In order to control the transmission quality on the Iu/A interface, the performance of IP paths is measured on the basis of Real-time Transport Protocol (RTP) and Real-time Transport Control Protocol (RTCP) packets. The transmission quality of the IP paths is related to the measurement items such as Round Trip Time (RTT) of packets, delay jitter of packets, and packet loss rate, In order to increase the ability of surveillance.The item is measured only when the RTCP function is switched on.
