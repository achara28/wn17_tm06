EFACH Measurement per cell(EFACH.Cell)

Overview

CELL_FACH state user can use HSDSCH channel as transfer channel after R7 protocol.

EFACH refers to the status that the UE uplink is established on the RACH and the UE downlink is established on the HSDSCH. This technology can increase CELL_FACH user downlink data peak rate and shorten state transfer delay time. The measurement unit EFACH Measurement (Cell) is used to measure this technology related services on the basis of Cell.
