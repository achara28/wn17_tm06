Overview

Radio Bearer (RB) is the service provided by Layer 2 for transfer of user data between UE and UTRAN. The RB procedures include:RB setup procedures, RB reconfiguration procedures, RB release procedures, Transport channel reconfiguration procedures and Physical channel reconfiguration procedure.
